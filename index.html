Overview

TapClank is an open-world sandbox game which grants you the creative freedom to build and explore your own creations. Draw structures, build buildings, and whatever other thing that arises from your imagination.

Controls

- A,D or left and right arrow keys to move
- Space to jump
- G to switch between build/erase mode
- click/tap and hold to draw or erase materials
- ESC to pause game




Note: the game is currently in pre-alpha, and may contain bugs.
